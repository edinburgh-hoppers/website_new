About Us

As the official society for Gender Minorities in Informatics at the University of Edinburgh, our goal is to cultivate an open community with the aim to provide a supportive and inclusive society where people are comfortable and can build both technical and networking skills to thrive in the tech environment.

We differ from other societies at the University of Edinburgh in that every woman, transgender and non-binary person studying or working at the School of Informatics is automatically considered as part of Hoppers community, and since from this year Hoppers society became one of EUSA societies, people showing interest in our society need to obtain free Hoppers membership tickets through the link below to verify their membership from technical side.
